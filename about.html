About the Club

Flagro Art Club is the official creative community at IISER Berhampur, bridging the gap between scientific inquiry and artistic expression. We provide a dynamic, inclusive space for students to explore their imagination through a fusion of art, culture, and discovery.

Our club thrives through specialized teams the Design Team crafts our visual identity, the Social Media Team drives community engagement, and the Events Team curates immersive workshops and exhibitions. Together, these groups create a platform for members to showcase their talents and share their unique visions.

A signature highlight of Flagro is Pictopia, our dedicated section for comics and visual storytelling. By fostering dialogue and collaboration, we celebrate the power of creativity and provide a home for every artist, designer, and storyteller to grow.

Creative Division

Design Division

The Design Division focuses on graphic design, digital illustration, branding, poster design, and visual storytelling.

Members contribute to event posters, digital illustrations, creative campaigns, and UI design projects that shape the visual identity of Flagro and campus events.

This team transforms ideas into stunning visuals across multiple mediums.

Creative Division

Pictopia

The Comic Division specializes in storytelling through comics, humor, and character design.

Members create comic strips, character illustrations, meme art, and narrative panels that combine creativity with engaging visual narratives.

Pictopia produces compelling stories through sequential art.
