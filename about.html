About the Club

Flagro Art Club is the official creative community at IISER Berhampur dedicated to promoting artistic expression across multiple mediums including traditional art, digital design, comics, and visual storytelling.

The club organizes workshops, exhibitions, competitions, and creative events that help students explore their imagination and share their work with the community.

Flagro serves as a space where creativity meets collaboration, bringing together artists, designers, and storytellers.

Creative Division

Design Division

The Design Division focuses on graphic design, digital illustration, branding, poster design, and visual storytelling.

Members contribute to event posters, digital illustrations, creative campaigns, and UI design projects that shape the visual identity of Flagro and campus events.

This team transforms ideas into stunning visuals across multiple mediums.

Creative Division

Comic Division

The Comic Division specializes in storytelling through comics, humor, and character design.

Members create comic strips, character illustrations, meme art, and narrative panels that combine creativity with engaging visual narratives.

Pictopia produces compelling stories through sequential art.
